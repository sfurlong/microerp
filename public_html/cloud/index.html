Altaprise

Who We Are

Altaprise is a start-up consulting firm focused on serving the comprehensive needs of commercial enterprises and US Federal Government agencies in the full range of cloud advisory and implementation services. Alatprise will initially target Oracle Cloud-based technology deployments and then opportunistically move to providing services on other cloud technologies during subsequent growth phases. Altaprise has a core team of professionals who have deep Oracle Cloud expertise. It is launching its venture now to leverage the recent release of Oracle's Cloud offerings and the launch of major Oracle sales campaigns to move its North American customer base to its cloud platforms. Altaprise will leverage its skills and network to participate in this new market and establish itself as a premiere Oracle Cloud services vendor.
