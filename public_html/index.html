microERP is a free, open-source, web-based Accounting/ERP system. Built in Java using the best-of-breed Open Source components. microERP offers a solid, customizable, secure platform for your business.

Main Features

  • Sales - unlimited customers, customer branches, invoicing, credits, payments and refunds, sales areas, sales types, sales persons, shipping companies.
  • Purchasing - unlimited suppliers, supplier price lists, different payment terms.
  • Items and Inventory - inventory and non-inventory items, item categories, inventory locations,adjustments, location transfers, reorder levels.
  • Manufacturing - Bill of materials, work centre tracking, advanced work order entry.
  • Banking - Multiple bank accounts, detailed payment and deposit, currencies, exchange rates, bank inquiry and reports.
  • General Ledger - unlimited accounts, account groups, manual journal entry, detailed inquiry and reports.
  • Access and Customization - Multi-user access with different priviliges, transaction voiding, transaction reprinting, backup and restore, multiple taxes.

microERP is currently in Production development state. Enhancements are added often or can be requested.
